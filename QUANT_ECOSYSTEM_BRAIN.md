QUANT ECOSYSTEM 3.0
Institutional Autonomous Quant Trading Ecosystem

Author: Balaji
Purpose: Build a hedge-fund grade autonomous trading desk.


----------------------------------------------------
PROJECT OBJECTIVE
----------------------------------------------------

Quant Ecosystem 3.0 is a fully autonomous institutional trading system capable of:

• Multi-asset trading
• Multi-broker integration
• Strategy ingestion
• Strategy evaluation
• Autonomous execution
• Risk governance
• Portfolio management
• Research and development
• Remote control
• AI assistance

The ecosystem must survive any market condition.

Decisions are strictly statistical and mathematical.

No emotional trading.
